Refund Policy

Last Updated: December 6, 2024

Simple Summary: We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with our service for any reason, you can request a full refund within 7 days of purchase.

1. Refund Eligibility

Eligible for Refund:

  • Quarterly Subscription ($7.99/3 months): Full refund within 7 days of purchase

Not Eligible for Refund:

  • Requests made after 7 days from the purchase date
  • Accounts that have violated our Terms of Service
  • Repeated refund requests (abuse of refund policy)

2. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Email us at ladyiney25@gmail.com
  2. Subject line: "Refund Request - [Your Order Number]"
  3. Include:
    • Your email address used for purchase
    • Order number or transaction ID
    • Purchase date
    • Reason for refund (optional but appreciated)

No questions asked: You don't need to provide a reason, but feedback helps us improve our service.

3. Refund Processing Time

  • Review: 1-2 business days

Total time: Expect to see the refund in your account within 7-14 business days from approval.

4. Refund Method

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase:

  • Credit/Debit Card: Refunded to the same card
  • PayPal: Refunded to your PayPal account
  • Other payment methods: Refunded to the original source

5. After Refund

Once your refund is processed:

  • You will no longer receive daily Bible verse emails

  • You can re-subscribe at any time

6. Cancellation vs. Refund

Cancellation (No Refund):

  • You keep access until the end of the paid period
  • No future charges

Refund (Within 7 Days):

  • Full refund of your purchase
  • Immediate cancellation of subscription
  • Immediate loss of access

7. Special Circumstances

Technical Issues:

If you experience technical problems that prevent you from using our service, please contact us immediately. We will:

  • Work to resolve the issue quickly
  • Extend your subscription if needed
  • Offer a refund if we cannot resolve the issue

Billing Errors:

If you were charged incorrectly, we will issue a full refund regardless of the 7-day window.

8. Fair Use Policy

We trust our customers, but we reserve the right to refuse refunds in cases of:
